5. Delivery Issues & Support

If You Don't Receive Access

Common Issues & Solutions:

1. Email Not Received (most common):

  • Check spam/junk folder
  • Check promotions tab (Gmail)
  • Wait 10-15 minutes for email delivery
  • Add info@neuverrax.com to your contacts

2. Login Issues:

  • Use "Forgot Password" to reset
  • Ensure you're using the correct email address
  • Clear browser cache and cookies
  • Try a different browser or device

3. Payment Processing Delays:

  • Some banks may take 5-10 minutes to confirm payment
  • Check your payment confirmation email
  • Verify charge appeared on your account

Contacting Support

If you experience delivery issues:

  • Email: support@neuverrax.com
  • Response Time: Within 24 hours (typically within 4 business hours)
  • Include: Your registered email, purchase confirmation, and description of issue
